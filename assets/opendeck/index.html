What's New · v2.1.3

Major stability and presenter updates shipped in this release:

1. Presenter Sync Rebuild

Presenter Assistant now syncs more reliably between the main deck and Speaker View.

2. Speaker View Controls

Added slide jump list, focus mode, notes controls, and a manual timer with reset/targets.

3. Template Capacity Limits

Repeatable sections now enforce limits to keep slides readable across different displays.

4. Limit UX Improvements

Add buttons disable at capacity and inline guidance appears only when the limit is reached.

5. Laptop Rendering Stability

Autofit and 4-card Feature Grid behavior were tuned for improved small-screen stability.

1. 100% Local Storage

OpenDeck is a "Vanilla+" local-first application. Your presentations, slides, and settings are saved exclusively in your browser's LocalStorage. We do not transmit, save, or have access to your presentation data or any content you create.

2. Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to measure website traffic and performance. This is a privacy-first analytics service that does not use cookies or track individual users across the web.
